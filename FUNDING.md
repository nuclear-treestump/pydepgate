# Funding

pydepgate is public defensive infrastructure for Python supply-chain security.

It is built and maintained by one person. If pydepgate saves your team time, strengthens your CI/CD pipeline, helps review a suspicious package, or prevents an incident, sponsorship helps keep the project maintained, independent, and available to the commons.

Sponsorship supports:

1. Continued development of pydepgate and its documentation.
2. Infrastructure for planned systems such as evidence collection, Warehouse, Workplan, threat feeds, and burn notices.
3. Time spent triaging issues, reviewing reports, improving detections, and maintaining release security.
4. Future compensation for additional maintainers if the project grows beyond one person.

If pydepgate provided value to you or your organization, consider sponsoring development through the Sponsors button or Tidelift.
