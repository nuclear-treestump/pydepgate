# Funding

pydepgate is public defensive infrastructure for Python supply-chain security.

It is built and maintained by one person. If pydepgate saves your team time, strengthens your CI/CD pipeline, helps review a suspicious package, or prevents an incident, sponsorship helps keep the project maintained, independent, and available to the commons.

Sponsorship supports:

1. Continued development of pydepgate and its documentation.
2. Infrastructure for planned systems such as evidence collection, Warehouse, Workplan, threat feeds, and burn notices.
3. Time spent triaging issues, reviewing reports, improving detections, and maintaining release security.
4. Future compensation for additional maintainers if the project grows beyond one person.

If pydepgate provided value to you or your organization, consider sponsoring development through the Sponsors button or Tidelift.

SLA, emergency response retainer, or purchase of roadmap control unless
separately agreed in writing.

## One-Time Sponsorship

Roadmap sponsorship helps accelerate public pydepgate development.

Sponsored roadmap work still lands in the public project. Sponsorship does
not buy exclusivity, private ownership, roadmap control, or suppression of
general defensive improvements.

### Roadmap Sponsor - $250

Help accelerate a pydepgate roadmap item.

This tier is intended for individuals, small teams, and organizations that
want to support a specific public feature without creating a custom private
engagement.

Includes:

* public credit for helping accelerate the roadmap item;
* sponsor input considered during feature design;
* pre-release access for testing and feedback for up to 14 days when
  practical;
* credit in the relevant issue, pull request, release notes, or sponsor
  listing when appropriate.

Notes:

* Final design and implementation decisions remain with the maintainer.
* General defensive improvements, detection logic, parser hardening, and
  security fixes may be released publicly at the maintainer’s discretion.
